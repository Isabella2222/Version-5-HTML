Information

Welcome to the Information page! Here you will find many ways to help with keeping the environment clean.

  1. Picking up rubbish! Picking up rubbish is probably the easiest way to keep our area clean. By picking up a few bits of rubbish each day, we can slowley clean up our environment and make it a clean, tidy space to be in.
  2. Reducing your plastic! Plastic is a big issue with pollution and litter as it doesn't biodegrade, which means it stays around for everyone to see. By reducing the amount of plastic you bring to school, you can help keep our area clean.
  3. Encourage your friends! Without the help of LOTS of people, the rubbish issue our community faces will never be solved. Go pick up some rubbish with your friends, and help make our community better.
